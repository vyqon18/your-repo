GERC hearing (Petition No. 2612/2026) could unlock the commercial phase of Phase-VII standalone BESS in Gujarat

I’m sharing this because it signals a key regulatory step that could unlock the commercial phase of a large battery-storage buildout in Gujarat — a move many investors and developers are watching closely.

What’s happening

The Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC) has scheduled an official hearing (Petition No. 2612/2026) to consider a petition under Section 63/86 of the Electricity Act seeking adoption of the tariff discovered in the competitive bidding for the Phase-VII standalone Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) tender issued by Gujarat Urja Vikas Nigam Limited (GUVNL) (RfS No. GUVNL/BESS/Phase VII dated 23 Jul 2025). This hearing was listed for 4 Feb 2026 on the regulator’s site.

The petition asks the Commission to formally approve the tariff that emerged from the reverse auction process — a crucial procedural step required under the Electricity Act when procuring power or storage through competitive bidding.

Key takeaways

Regulatory milestone Tariff adoption is the bridge between bid discovery and contract execution.
Project bankability Adopted tariff helps lock revenue assumptions needed for financial close.
Watch the order The post-hearing order will indicate when Phase-VII winners can move to execution.

Why it matters for developers & investors

In past phases of BESS procurement in Gujarat, once GERC has adopted the tariff, the procurer must sign the Battery Energy Storage Purchase Agreements (BESPAs) and project executors can move toward financial close and commissioning — locking in revenue assumptions for the awarded contracts.

Tracking GERC’s order post-hearing will be key for developers and investors to understand when the Phase-VII winners can finalize financing and contracts.

  • Developer impact: clarity on executable contracts (BESPAs/PPAs) and timelines.
  • Investor impact: stronger confidence in cashflow assumptions, enabling financial close.
  • Market signal: progress on storage procurement mechanism and implementation in Gujarat.
